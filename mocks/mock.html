Where to Put It
✦ High-Yield Savings (HYSA)
4–5% APY. Your money earns interest while it sits. Best for emergency fund. Marcus, Ally, SoFi are all free to open.
Regular Savings
Where you are now. Earns ~0.01% APY. Fine for holding cash, but your money isn't growing.
CDs (Certificates of Deposit)
Lock money for 6–12 months at ~5%. Good once you have a 3-month buffer locked in a HYSA first.
⚠ Checking Account
Too easy to spend. Separate account = psychological barrier = meaningfully higher savings rate over time.
